Biking Culture

Beyond Barriers

Support Structure

Branding & Identity

The biking culture is a passionate lifestyle where individuals are part of the community culling members from commerce, finance, manufacturing and the public sector.

They have built a support structure that involves encouraging safety, health and family through ensuring safety equipment is used, supporting charities and facilitating the rebranding of the 'bad boy' image of bikers to one that encompasses desirable elements of society.

The Bikers Wearhouse label further seeks to enhance this spirit of freedom, ambition, achievement and the ability to think outside the box, live beyond barriers and reach for the sky by providing apparel that reflects these ideals.
